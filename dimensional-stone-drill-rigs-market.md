They are suitable for hard rock surfaces. On the other hand, Mechanical drill rigs rely on mechanical power, making them more economical but less precise than hydraulic rigs. They are typically used for softer rock surfaces. Both types of drill rigs play a crucial role in the mining and construction industries by efficiently drilling holes in dimensional stones.</p></p>
