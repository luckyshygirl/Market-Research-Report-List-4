<p><strong>The Industrial Steam Turbines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reaction Turbines</li><li>Impulse Turbines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Steam Turbines are classified into two main types: Reaction Turbines and Impulse Turbines. Reaction turbines utilize the pressure drop across the turbine blades to generate power, while impulse turbines rely on the kinetic energy of the incoming steam to drive the turbine blades. Both types play a crucial role in various industrial applications where steam power generation is required. Reaction turbines are more commonly used in high-pressure and high-power applications, while impulse turbines are often preferred for lower pressure and smaller scale operations.</p></p>
