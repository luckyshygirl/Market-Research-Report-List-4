<p><p>Automatic ducting machines are categorized into different types based on their fabrication methods. Fabricated automatic ducting machines are made by joining duct components together to form the final product. Flanged automatic ducting machines have flanges attached to the ducts for easy installation and sealing. Abricated automatic ducting machines are made by bending or shaping duct materials. Other types of automatic ducting machines may include unique fabrication methods or specialized features not covered by the other categories. These different types cater to a variety of needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
