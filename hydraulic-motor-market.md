<p><p>Hydraulic motors are classified into different types based on their design and operation. The three main categories are vane type, plunger type, and others. Vane type hydraulic motors use rotating vanes to convert hydraulic fluid energy into mechanical energy. Plunger type motors feature pistons that move back and forth to produce rotational motion. The category of other hydraulic motors includes radial piston motors, gear motors, and screw motors. Each type has unique features and applications based on their design and performance characteristics.</p></p>
