<p><p>Servo Drive Units are electronic devices used to control the motion of a servo motor. There are two main types of Servo Drive Units: Analog Servo Drivers and Digital Servo Drivers. Analog Servo Drivers use analog signals to control the motor's speed and position, while Digital Servo Drivers use digital signals for more precise and efficient control. Digital Servo Drivers have become more popular due to their higher accuracy, faster response times, and better communication capabilities compared to Analog Servo Drivers.</p></p>
