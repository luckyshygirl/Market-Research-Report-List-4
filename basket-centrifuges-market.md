<p><p>Basket centrifuges are classified into two main types based on the orientation of the basket: vertical and horizontal. Vertical basket centrifuges operate with the basket mounted upright, allowing for sedimentation to take place vertically. Horizontal basket centrifuges, on the other hand, have the basket oriented horizontally, promoting a more efficient separation process. Both types are commonly used in industries such as chemical, pharmaceutical, and food processing for tasks like liquid-solid separation, filtration, and extraction.</p></p>
