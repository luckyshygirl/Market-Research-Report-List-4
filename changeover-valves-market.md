<p><p>Changeover valves are devices that allow for switching between different flow paths or channels. There are three main types of changeover valves - electric, manual, and pneumatic.</p><p>Electric changeover valves are controlled by electrical signals, allowing for automated switching between flow paths. Manual changeover valves require manual manipulation to switch between channels. Pneumatic changeover valves are controlled by air pressure, making them suitable for applications where electric power is not available or practical.</p><p>Each type of changeover valve offers unique benefits and advantages depending on the specific application.</p></p>
