<p><ul><li>Manual Trigger</li><li>Thermal Trigger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kitchen Hood Fire Suppression Systems have two main types in the market - Manual Trigger and Thermal Trigger. Manual Trigger systems require human intervention to activate, usually through a pull station or button. Thermal Trigger systems, on the other hand, automatically detect high temperatures and activate the suppression system without the need for human intervention. Both types are crucial for fire safety in commercial kitchens, but thermal trigger systems provide an added layer of protection by acting swiftly without relying on human response.</p></p>
