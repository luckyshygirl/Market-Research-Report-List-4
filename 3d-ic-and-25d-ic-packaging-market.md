<p><p>3D IC packaging involves stacking multiple integrated circuits (ICs) on top of each other to increase performance and reduce size. This can be achieved through 3D wafer-level chip-scale packaging, which involves stacking individual dies on a wafer level. 3D Through-Silicon Via (TSV) technology allows for vertical connections between stacked ICs, enabling faster data transfer. 2.5D IC packaging involves placing multiple dies side by side on an interposer, enabling high bandwidth communication between the dies. These technologies are driving advancements in compact and high-performance electronic devices.</p></p>
