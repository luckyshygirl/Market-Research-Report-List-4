<p><p>Electric Vehicle UBI market types include Pay-As-You-Drive (PAYD), Pay-How-You-Drive (PHYD), and Manage-How-You-Drive (MHYD). PAYD charges drivers based on the distance they drive, PHYD factors in driving behavior such as speed and braking habits, and MHYD combines both distance and behavior metrics to determine insurance rates. These market types aim to promote safer driving practices, reduce emissions, and incentivize drivers to use electric vehicles by offering personalized insurance rates based on driving habits.</p></p>
