<p><ul><li>On-Pump Transfusion Device</li><li>Off-Pump Transfusion Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cardiopulmonary auto-transfusion systems are utilized in cardiac surgeries for collecting and filtering blood from the surgical site, and then re-infusing it back into the patient. The two main types of these systems are on-pump transfusion devices and off-pump transfusion devices. On-pump devices are integrated into the heart-lung machine used during surgeries where the heart is stopped. Off-pump devices, on the other hand, operate independently of the heart-lung machine and are used in surgeries where the heart continues to beat during the procedure.</p></p>
