<p><p>Traditional probe cards are testing tools used in the semiconductor industry to evaluate the electrical performance of integrated circuits (ICs) during the manufacturing process. These probe cards consist of a probe head with many tiny needles that make contact with the ICs on a wafer, allowing electrical signals to be tested.</p><p>The traditional probe cards market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.30% during the forecast period. The growth of this market is driven by the increasing demand for consumer electronics, such as smartphones and tablets, which require high-performance ICs. Additionally, the growing use of IoT devices and the development of advanced technologies like 5G are also contributing to the demand for traditional probe cards.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the traditional probe cards market is the development of advanced probe card technologies, such as cantilever probe cards and vertical probe cards, which offer improved performance and reliability. <p><strong>The Traditional Probe Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blade/Tungsten Probe Cards</li><li>Epoxy/Cantilever Probe Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blade/Tungsten probe cards are a type of traditional probe card that uses blades or tungsten needles to make contact with the pins on a semiconductor wafer. These probe cards are known for their durability and long lifespan. On the other hand, Epoxy/Cantilever probe cards use epoxy or cantilever structures for the contacting mechanism. They are popular for their high precision and accuracy in testing semiconductor devices. Both types of probe cards are widely used in the semiconductor industry for testing and inspection purposes.</p></p>
