<p><p>Bone conduction hearing implants come in different types to suit individual preferences and needs. Behind the Ear (BTE) implants sit comfortably behind the ear, while In the Ear (ITE) implants are customized to fit inside the outer ear. In the Canal (ITC) implants are placed partially inside the ear canal, offering a more discreet option. Completely In Canal (CIC) implants are the smallest and most discreet, fitting completely inside the ear canal for a natural look. Each type offers unique benefits for users.</p></p>
