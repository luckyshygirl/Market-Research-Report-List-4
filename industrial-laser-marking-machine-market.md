<p><p>Industrial laser marking machines are categorized into four main types based on the laser technology they use: Fiber Laser Marking Machine, CO2 Laser Marking Machine, UV Laser Marking Machine, and Others. Fiber laser marking machines are known for their high precision and speed, while CO2 laser marking machines are commonly used for marking non-metal materials. UV laser marking machines are preferred for applications requiring high resolution and minimal heat generation. The Others category includes machines that use different laser technologies such as green or infrared lasers.</p></p>
