<p><ul><li>S-Lay Installation</li><li>J-Lay Installation</li><li>Drag-In Installation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Subsea Pipeline Engineering Services market is segmented into three main types based on installation methods: S-Lay Installation, J-Lay Installation, and Drag-In Installation. S-Lay Installation involves laying the pipeline on the seabed in a snake-like manner, while J-Lay Installation involves lowering the pipeline from a vertical tower on a vessel. Drag-In Installation involves dragging the pipeline under the water by a vessel. Different methods are chosen based on factors like water depth, soil conditions, and project requirements.</p></p>
