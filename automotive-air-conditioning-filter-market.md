<p><ul><li>Particle filter</li><li>Activated carbon filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Air Conditioning Filter market includes two main types: Particle filter and Activated carbon filter. Particle filters are designed to trap and remove particles such as dust, pollen, and other pollutants from the air in the car. Activated carbon filters, on the other hand, are specifically designed to absorb and neutralize odors, harmful gases, and VOCs in the air. Both filters are essential for maintaining clean and odor-free air inside the vehicle, providing a healthier and more comfortable driving experience for passengers.</p></p>
