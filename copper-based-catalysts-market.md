<p><p>Copper-based catalysts come in two main types: powder and lumpy. Powder catalysts are finely ground copper particles that have a high surface area, making them ideal for gas-phase reactions and catalytic converters. Lumpy catalysts, on the other hand, consist of larger copper particles that are used in liquid-phase reactions and industrial processes. Both types of catalysts have their own unique applications and are essential for driving chemical reactions in various industries.</p></p>
