<p><p>Residential HVAC air filters come in two main types: pleated HVAC air filters and panel HVAC air filters. Pleated filters are more efficient at trapping smaller particles due to their larger surface area, resulting in better air quality and improved HVAC system performance. Panel filters are less expensive and typically need to be replaced more often, but still provide adequate filtration for most residential applications. Both types of filters play a crucial role in maintaining clean indoor air and protecting HVAC equipment from damage.</p></p>
