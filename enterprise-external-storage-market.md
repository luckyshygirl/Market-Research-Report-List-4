<p><p>Enterprise external storage market includes All Flash Array (AFA) and Hybrid Flash Array (HFA) types. AFA is designed for high-performance storage needs, utilizing flash storage for faster data access and processing. It is ideal for applications requiring low latency and high IOPS. HFA combines flash and traditional hard disk drives, offering a balance between performance and cost-effectiveness. It is suitable for workloads that require a mix of high performance and capacity storage. Both AFA and HFA cater to different enterprise storage requirements based on performance and cost considerations.</p></p>
