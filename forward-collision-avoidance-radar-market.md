<p><p>Forward Collision Avoidance Radar systems are classified into three main types: Ultrasonic Radar, Laser Radar, and Microwave Radar. Ultrasonic Radar uses sound waves to detect objects in front of the vehicle, while Laser Radar uses laser beams for detection. Microwave Radar operates by emitting radio waves and analyzing their reflections to determine the presence of obstacles. Each type of radar system has its own advantages and limitations, leading to a diverse market with options catering to various needs and preferences in collision avoidance technology.</p></p>
