<p><p>Passive RF sensors come in three different types based on the frequency they operate at - Low Frequency RF Sensors, Mid Frequency RF Sensors, and High Frequency RF Sensors. Low frequency sensors typically operate below 100 kHz and are used for long-range communication and sensing. Mid-frequency sensors operate between 100 kHz and 1 GHz and are commonly used in applications such as wireless charging and proximity sensing. High frequency sensors operate above 1 GHz and are used in radar systems and high-speed data transmission applications.</p></p>
