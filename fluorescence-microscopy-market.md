<p><p>Fluorescence microscopy market consists of two main types: Upright Fluorescence Microscopy and Inverted Fluorescence Microscopy. </p><p>Upright Fluorescence Microscopy involves observing samples from above, which is ideal for thicker specimens or when using specialized imaging techniques. In contrast, Inverted Fluorescence Microscopy allows for imaging samples from underneath, making it suitable for live cell imaging and studying cells cultured on the bottom of a dish. Both types play a crucial role in biological research, medical diagnostics, and materials science.</p></p>
