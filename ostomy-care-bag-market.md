<p><p>The ostomy care bag market offers three main types of products: one-piece systems, two-piece systems, and skin barriers. One-piece systems are convenient and easy to use, as the bag and skin barrier are connected as one unit. Two-piece systems allow for the separate changing of the bag and skin barrier, providing flexibility and customization. Skin barriers are essential for protecting the skin around the stoma from irritation and leakage. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of ostomy patients.</p></p>
