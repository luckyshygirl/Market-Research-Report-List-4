<p><p>Coloured glass is available in various types, with two of the most common being coloured float glass and coloured plain plate glass. Coloured float glass is created by adding metal oxides to molten glass during the manufacturing process to achieve vibrant hues. On the other hand, coloured plain plate glass involves a similar process but is produced in sheets with a flat surface. Both markets cater to different aesthetic preferences and have applications in architecture, interior design, and automotive industries.</p></p>
