<p><ul><li>Open Headbox</li><li>Air Cushion Headbox</li><li>Hydraulic Headbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Headboxes are crucial components in papermaking machines, responsible for distributing the pulp evenly onto the forming wire. The three main types of headboxes are Open Headbox, Air Cushion Headbox, and Hydraulic Headbox. Open Headboxes are traditional and widely used, providing a simple design for pulp distribution. Air Cushion Headboxes utilize air pressure to create a cushion that ensures uniform distribution. Hydraulic Headboxes use hydraulic pressure to control the pulp flow, allowing for precise adjustments and improved paper quality. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the papermaking industry.</p></p>
