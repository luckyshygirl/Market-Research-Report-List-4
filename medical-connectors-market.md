<p><p>Medical connectors market can be divided into two main types: Embedded Type and Push-Pull Type. Embedded Type connectors are integrated directly into medical devices, providing a permanent and secure connection. On the other hand, Push-Pull Type connectors are designed for quick and easy connection and disconnection, making them ideal for use in applications where frequent changes are required. Both types of connectors play a crucial role in ensuring reliable and efficient communication between medical devices and equipment.</p></p>
