<p><p>Fastening power tools can be categorized into different types based on their power source. Electric fastening power tools can be further divided into corded and cordless varieties. Corded electric tools are reliable and powerful, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Pneumatic fastening power tools, also known as air tools, use compressed air to drive fasteners. They are typically lightweight and have high power-to-weight ratios, making them ideal for professional use.</p></p>
