<p><p>Bolted bonnet check valves are commonly available in two main market types based on their connections: socket weld and flanged connections. Socket weld connections involve welding the valve to the pipe, providing a strong and leak-proof connection. Flanged connections use bolted flanges to secure the valve to the pipe, allowing for easier installation and maintenance. Both types are widely used in various industries where flow control and prevention of backflow are essential.</p></p>
