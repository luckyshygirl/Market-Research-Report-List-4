<p><p>Feed mycotoxin detoxifiers market includes mycotoxin binders and mycotoxin modifiers. Mycotoxin binders are substances that bind to mycotoxins in the gastrointestinal tract, preventing their absorption and reducing their harmful effects. Mycotoxin modifiers, on the other hand, work by altering the chemical structure of mycotoxins to make them less toxic. Both types of detoxifiers are essential in animal feed to protect livestock from the negative impacts of mycotoxin contamination.</p></p>
