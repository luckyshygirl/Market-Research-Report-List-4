<p><p>Oil and Gas Drilling Bits Market consists of three main types - Fixed Cutter, Roller Cone, and Other. Fixed Cutter bits have diamond or other cutting structures fixed onto the bit body, suitable for hard formations. Roller Cone bits have cones with teeth that roll on the bottom of the hole, effective for soft to medium formations. Other bits include hybrid, PDC, and natural diamond bits, offering specialized solutions for specific drilling challenges. Each type caters to different geological conditions and drilling requirements in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
