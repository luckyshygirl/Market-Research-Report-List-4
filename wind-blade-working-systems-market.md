<p><p>Wind Blade Working Systems are divided into two main types: Horizontal Axis and Vertical Axis systems. The Horizontal Axis systems consist of blades that rotate on a horizontal plane, while the Vertical Axis systems have blades that rotate on a vertical plane. Horizontal Axis systems are more commonly used in commercial wind turbines due to their higher efficiency and power output. Vertical Axis systems, on the other hand, are often used in smaller-scale applications and are known for their ability to capture wind from any direction.</p></p>
