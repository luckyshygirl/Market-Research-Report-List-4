<p><p>Radiosurgery and Radiotherapy Robotics market is segmented into three types based on the type of radiation used for treatment: X-ray Based, Gamma-ray Based, and Proton-beam Based. X-ray Based treatment utilizes high-energy X-rays to target and destroy cancer cells. Gamma-ray Based treatment involves the use of gamma rays, which are typically emitted by Cobalt-60 or other radioactive isotopes. Proton-beam Based treatment uses high-energy protons to precisely target tumors while minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue. Each type of treatment offers unique benefits and is used based on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
