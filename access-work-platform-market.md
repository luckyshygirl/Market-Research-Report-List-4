<p><p>Access work platforms are commonly categorized into types based on their design and functionality. Telescoping boom lifts offer vertical and horizontal reach with a single arm extension. Articulated boom lifts have multiple sections that can move independently, allowing for greater maneuverability around obstacles. Scissor lifts are vertical platforms that extend upward on crisscrossing supports. Truck-mounted lifts are attached to vehicles for easy transportation. Other types of access work platforms may include mast lifts, material lifts, and personnel lifts, each serving different purposes in the industry.</p></p>
