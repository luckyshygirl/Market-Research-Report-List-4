<p><p>Benchtop dissolved oxygen meters come in two main types: single input and dual input. Single input meters are designed to measure the dissolved oxygen level in a single sample at a time, making them ideal for smaller-scale operations or research. Dual input meters, on the other hand, have the capability to measure dissolved oxygen levels in two separate samples simultaneously, allowing for more efficient and streamlined testing processes. Both types of meters are important tools for monitoring water quality in various industries and research settings.</p></p>
