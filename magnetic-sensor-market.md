<p><p>Magnetic sensor market is segmented into different types including Hall Effect Type, AMR Type, GMR Type, and other. The Hall Effect Type sensors are based on the principle of Hall Effect and are widely used in various applications. AMR Type sensors use the Anisotropic Magneto-Resistance effect for detection. GMR Type sensors utilize the Giant Magneto-Resistance effect for high sensitivity. Other types in the market include magnetostrictive, SQUID, and fluxgate sensors, each offering unique characteristics and applications in industries like automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
