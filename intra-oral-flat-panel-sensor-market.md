<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intra-oral flat panel sensor market consists of two main types: CCD and CMOS sensors. CCD sensors use a series of capacitors to convert light into electronic signals, offering high image quality and sensitivity. On the other hand, CMOS sensors utilize individual pixels to capture light, providing lower power consumption and faster readout speeds. Both types are widely used in dental imaging for capturing high-resolution intra-oral X-ray images, with CCD sensors being preferred for their superior image quality and CMOS sensors for their efficiency and speed.</p></p>
