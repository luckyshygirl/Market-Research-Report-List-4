<p><p>Electric energy meters are devices used to measure the amount of electricity consumed by a household or business. There are two main types of electric energy meters: single-phase and three-phase meters. Single-phase meters are used in residential settings where power is provided through a single voltage source. Three-phase meters are used in industrial and commercial settings where power is provided through three separate voltage sources. Both types of meters are essential for accurately monitoring and billing electricity consumption.</p></p>
