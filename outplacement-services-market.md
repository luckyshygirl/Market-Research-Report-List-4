<p><p>Outplacement services market can be categorized into two types – Group Outplacement and Personal Outplacement. Personal Outplacement, on the other hand, is tailored for individual employees who have lost their jobs, providing personalized job search assistance, career coaching, and networking opportunities. Both types of outplacement services aim to help employees transition smoothly into new roles and minimize the impact of job loss.</p></p>
