<p><p>Medical imaging sensor market can be categorized into two main types: Spatial Scanning Sensors and Spectral Scanning Sensors. Spatial Scanning Sensors are designed to capture detailed images of a specific area of the body, providing high-resolution visuals that are crucial for accurate diagnosis. On the other hand, Spectral Scanning Sensors are able to capture images based on the wavelengths of light, allowing for more comprehensive and detailed analysis of tissues and organs. Both types of sensors play a vital role in the field of medical imaging, aiding in the detection and treatment of various health conditions.</p></p>
