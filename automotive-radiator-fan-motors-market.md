<p><p>Automotive radiator fan motors are categorized into two main types: mechanical type and electric type. Mechanical fan motors are driven by the engine's crankshaft via a belt or a clutch, while electric fan motors are powered by electricity. Mechanical fan motors are simple and reliable, but electric fan motors are more efficient and provide better control over cooling needs. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, and the choice between them depends on factors such as vehicle design, cost, and performance requirements.</p></p>
