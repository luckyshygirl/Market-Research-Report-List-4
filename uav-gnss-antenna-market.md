<p><strong>The UAV GNSS Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Helix Antennas</li><li>Patch Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Helix antennas and patch antennas are two common types of UAV GNSS antennas used in navigation systems. Helix antennas are three-dimensional spiral antennas that provide circularly polarized radiation patterns, making them ideal for applications requiring high accuracy and reliability. Patch antennas, on the other hand, are flat and rectangular in shape, offering a more compact design suitable for smaller UAVs. Both antennas play a crucial role in ensuring precise positioning and navigation capabilities for unmanned aerial vehicles in various industries such as agriculture, defense, and transportation.</p></p>
