<p><p>Color Selector market can be divided into two main types - Chute Selector and Belt Selector. Chute Selector systems utilize a series of chutes to separate colored objects based on predetermined criteria, while Belt Selector systems use conveyor belts to transport colored objects to different destinations based on their color. Both types of systems are commonly used in industries such as recycling, textile manufacturing, and food processing to automate the sorting process and improve efficiency.</p></p>
