<p><p>Meat packaging equipment market includes various types of machines designed to package meat products efficiently and effectively. Vacuum chamber packaging machines are used to remove air from the packaging before sealing to extend the shelf life of the meat. Stretch film wrapping machines use plastic film to tightly wrap meat products for protection and preservation. Form/fill/seal packaging machines form the packaging, fill it with meat, and then seal it in one continuous process. Tray sealers are used to seal meat trays for retail display. Other types of equipment include labeling machines, portion cutters, and skin packaging machines.</p></p>
