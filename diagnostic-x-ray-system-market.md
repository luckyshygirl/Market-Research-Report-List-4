<p><p>Analog X-Ray Systems are traditional film-based systems that use photographic films to capture X-ray images. Digital X-Ray Systems use electronic sensors to capture and store images digitally, allowing for easier storage, retrieval, and manipulation of images. Retrofit X-Ray Systems are upgrades that can convert existing analog systems to digital systems, offering the benefits of digital technology without the need to replace the entire system. The market for these systems caters to healthcare facilities looking to enhance imaging capabilities and improve patient care.</p></p>
