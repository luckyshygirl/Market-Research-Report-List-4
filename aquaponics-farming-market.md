<p><p>Aquaponics farming is a sustainable farming method that combines aquaculture (the raising of fish) and hydroponics (the growing of plants in water) in a symbiotic environment. <p><p>Aquaponics farming involves the combination of aquaculture and hydroponics to create a sustainable farming method. Substrate cultivation involves using a solid medium for plant growth, deep-water raft cultivation uses floating rafts for plant growth in nutrient-rich water, nutrient film pipeline cultivation involves a thin layer of water flowing over plant roots, and aerosol cultivation uses a misting system to deliver nutrients to plants. Each market type offers unique benefits and challenges for those looking to engage in aquaponics farming.</p></p>
