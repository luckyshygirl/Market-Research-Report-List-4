<p><p>Smart street lighting systems are primarily categorized into two types: wired and wireless. Wired systems involve the use of physical cables to connect the street lights to a central control system, enabling real-time monitoring and control of the lighting infrastructure. On the other hand, wireless systems utilize communication technologies such as cellular networks or LoRa to establish connections between the street lights and the control system, offering more flexibility and scalability. Both types offer benefits such as improved energy efficiency, reduced maintenance costs, and enhanced safety.</p></p>
