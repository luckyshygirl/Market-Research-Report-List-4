<p><p>Dealer Management Systems (DMS) for auto dealerships can be classified into two main market types: Cloud-based and On-premise. Cloud-based DMS solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the system via the internet. On-premise DMS, on the other hand, are installed and operated on the dealership's own servers.
