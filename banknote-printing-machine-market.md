<p><p>Banknote-Printing Machine market is segmented into three types: Intaglio Printing, Offset Printing, and Others. Intaglio Printing involves raised or incised images being engraved onto the printing plate, resulting in high-quality, detailed banknotes. Offset Printing uses a lithographic process to transfer ink from a plate to a rubber blanket before being applied to the banknote. Other methods include digital printing and flexography. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different printing requirements in the banknote industry.</p></p>
