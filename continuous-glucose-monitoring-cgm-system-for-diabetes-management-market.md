<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) systems for diabetes management come in two main types: wearable and implantable. Wearable CGM systems are typically attached to the skin to continuously monitor glucose levels throughout the day, providing real-time data and alerts to the user. On the other hand, implantable CGM systems involve a sensor being inserted under the skin for long-term use, allowing for discreet and continuous monitoring without the need for frequent sensor changes. Both types of CGM systems offer valuable tools for managing diabetes effectively.</p></p>
