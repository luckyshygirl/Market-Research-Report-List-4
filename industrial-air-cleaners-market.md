Ion and ozone generators release ions or ozone to neutralize pollutants. Other types of air cleaners may include activated carbon filters, UV germicidal lamps, and photocatalytic oxidation filters. Each type offers unique benefits in improving indoor air quality in various industrial settings.</p></p>
