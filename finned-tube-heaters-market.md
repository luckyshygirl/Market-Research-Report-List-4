<p><p>Finned tube heaters are commonly available in two main types: Fin straight and Fin U shape. Fin straight heaters feature straight fins along the length of the tube, providing efficient heat transfer. On the other hand, Fin U shape heaters are designed with fins in a U shape, allowing for increased surface area and better heat distribution. Both types are popular choices for applications requiring high heat output and consistent performance. Customers can choose between these types based on their specific heating needs and preferences.</p></p>
