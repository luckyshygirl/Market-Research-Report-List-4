<p><p>Tyre testing market involves different types of testing methods for evaluating the performance and durability of tyres. Strength testing is done to assess the maximum load a tyre can withstand. Endurance testing measures a tyre's ability to withstand prolonged use and driving conditions. Rolling resistance testing evaluates the energy efficiency of a tyre by measuring the force required to keep it rolling. Other types of testing may include noise testing, wet grip testing, and winter performance testing to ensure overall quality and safety of tyres.</p></p>
