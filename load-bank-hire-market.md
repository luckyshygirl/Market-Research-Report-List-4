Resistive Load Banks are used to test power sources by converting electrical energy to heat. Reactive Load Banks are used to test power sources by creating inductive or capacitive loads. Resistive/Reactive Load Banks combine both functions in one unit. Each type serves a specific purpose in testing and maintaining the performance of power sources in various industries, such as manufacturing, data centers, and utilities.</p></p>
