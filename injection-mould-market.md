<p><strong>The Injection Mould Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-plate Injection Mold</li><li>Three-plate Injection Mould</li><li>Stack Injection Mold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injection mould market is divided into three main types: two-plate injection mould, three-plate injection mould, and stack injection mould. Two-plate injection mould is the simplest, with a core and cavity on each plate. Three-plate injection mould has an additional plate for the runner system, allowing for easier removal of the part. Stack injection mould is used for multi-component parts and involves stacking multiple moulds plates on top of each other. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the product being produced.</p></p>
