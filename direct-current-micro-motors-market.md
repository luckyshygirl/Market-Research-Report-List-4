<p><p>Direct current micro motors are commonly classified into two main types: brushless DC motors and brush DC motors. Brushless DC motors do not have brushes or commutators, leading to increased efficiency and durability. They are commonly used in applications requiring high performance and precision. On the other hand, brush DC motors have brushes and commutators, making them simpler and less expensive. They are ideal for applications with lower power requirements and cost limitations. Both types of motors play a crucial role in various industries, including automotive, robotics, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
