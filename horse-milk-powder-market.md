<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Horse Milk Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Freeze-dried Powder</li><li>Hot Dry Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horse Milk Powder is available in two main market types - Freeze-dried Powder and Hot Dry Powder. Freeze-dried Powder is obtained by freezing the liquid milk and then removing the ice through sublimation, resulting in a dry powder with a longer shelf life and better retention of nutrients. On the other hand, Hot Dry Powder is produced by evaporating the liquid milk at high temperatures, resulting in a dry powder with a slightly different taste and nutrient profile compared to freeze-dried powder. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
