<p><strong>The Industrial GigE Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Cameras</li><li>Line Scan Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial GigE cameras can be classified into two main types: Area scan cameras and Line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture a complete image of a scene at once, making them suitable for applications where the entire area needs to be analyzed, such as inspection or sorting. On the other hand, Line scan cameras capture images line by line, making them ideal for continuous inspection of long objects or surfaces, such as in web inspection or production lines. Each type has its own set of advantages and applications within the industrial imaging market.</p></p>
