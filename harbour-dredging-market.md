<p><p>Harbour dredging market types can be classified into four main categories: </p><p>1. Capital dredging involves creating new harbours or expanding existing ones to accommodate larger vessels and increase port capacity. </p><p>2. Coastal protection dredging is done to maintain and enhance coastal defenses, such as breakwaters and seawalls, to protect against erosion and storm surges. </p><p>3. Maintenance dredging involves regular removal of accumulated sediments to maintain navigable water depths and prevent clogging of harbours. </p><p>4. Rivers & Lakes dredging is carried out to improve water quality, enhance navigation, and manage flood control in inland water bodies.</p></p>
