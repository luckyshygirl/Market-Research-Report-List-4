<p><p>Cable circuit breakers are classified into three main types based on the voltage they can handle: high voltage circuit breakers, medium voltage circuit breakers, and low voltage circuit breakers. High voltage circuit breakers are used for voltages above 72.5 kV, medium voltage circuit breakers for voltages between 1 kV to 72.5 kV, and low voltage circuit breakers for voltages below 1 kV. Each type of circuit breaker is designed to protect electrical systems and equipment from overloads and short circuits specific to their voltage range.</p></p>
