<p><p>Permanent Monitoring Systems consist of sensors installed at specific points in electrical equipment to continuously monitor partial discharges. These systems provide real-time data and alerts to help prevent equipment failures. On the other hand, Temporary Monitoring Systems are used for short-term monitoring, such as during maintenance or diagnostic testing. These systems are portable and can be easily moved between different equipment.
