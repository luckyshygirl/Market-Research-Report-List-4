<p><p>Hand welding extruders are commonly used for various applications, with two main types being contact welding and hot gas welding. Contact welding is a process where the heated plastic material is pressed between two surfaces, creating a bond as it cools. On the other hand, hot gas welding involves using a heated gas to melt the plastic material and fuse it together. Both methods are widely used in industries such as construction, automotive, and plumbing for joining plastic components with precision and efficiency.</p></p>
