<p><p>The telemedicine stethoscope market can be categorized into two types: Wireless stethoscopes and Wired stethoscopes. Wireless stethoscopes utilize Bluetooth or other wireless technologies to transmit heart and lung sounds to a connected device, offering greater mobility and convenience for healthcare professionals. On the other hand, Wired stethoscopes have a physical connection to the device, ensuring a reliable and secure transmission of sound data. Both types of stethoscopes play a crucial role in remote patient consultations and monitoring in telemedicine applications.</p></p>
