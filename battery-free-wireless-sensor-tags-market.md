<p><p>Battery free wireless sensor tags market consists of three types: Active tags, Passive tags, and Semi-active tags. Active tags have a built-in power source and can actively broadcast signals over longer distances. Passive tags rely on external sources of power, such as RFID readers, to transmit data. Semi-active tags have a small power source to operate sensors and transmit data, but still rely on external sources for communication. Each tag type offers unique capabilities and is utilized in various industries for monitoring and tracking purposes.</p></p>
