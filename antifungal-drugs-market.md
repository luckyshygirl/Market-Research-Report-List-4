<p><strong>The Antifungal Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Azoles</li><li>Echinocandins</li><li>Polyenes</li><li>Allyamines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antifungal drugs market is categorized into four main types: Azoles, Echinocandins, Polyenes, and Allylamines. Azoles are the most commonly used antifungal drugs and work by interfering with fungal cell membrane formation. Echinocandins target the fungal cell wall, inhibiting its growth. Polyenes bind to fungal cell membranes, causing them to become porous and leading to cell death. Allylamines block an enzyme needed for fungal cell membrane synthesis. Each type of antifungal drug has specific mechanisms of action and targets different aspects of fungal growth.</p></p>
