<p><strong>The Energy and Sport Drinks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hypotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Isotonic Sports Drink</li><li>Hypertonic Sports Drink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Energy and sports drinks come in three main market types based on their concentration levels: hypotonic, isotonic, and hypertonic. Hypotonic sports drinks have a lower concentration of electrolytes and carbohydrates than the body, making them easier to absorb quickly. Isotonic sports drinks have a similar concentration to the body, making them effective for hydration and energy replenishment during exercise. Hypertonic sports drinks have a higher concentration of electrolytes and carbohydrates, making them ideal for post-exercise recovery and refueling.</p></p>
