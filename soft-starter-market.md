<p><p>Soft starters are used to reduce the starting current and torque of an electric motor, prolonging its lifespan and reducing stress on the electrical system. There are three main types of soft starter markets: mechanical, electronic, and mixed type. Mechanical soft starters use mechanical components to control the motor's starting current. Electronic soft starters use solid-state electronic components for more precise control. Mixed type soft starters combine elements of both mechanical and electronic systems for optimal performance and efficiency. Each type offers unique benefits and applications for various industries.</p></p>
