<p><p>Graphite column market types include packed column, bubble-cap column, sieve trays column, and others. Packed columns use a bed of solid packing material to increase surface area for contact between gas and liquid phases. Bubble-cap columns have perforated caps on each tray that allow gas bubbles to rise through the liquid. Sieve trays columns use trays with holes for liquid to flow through and promote contact between phases. Other types of columns may incorporate different designs or materials to achieve specific separation requirements.</p></p>
