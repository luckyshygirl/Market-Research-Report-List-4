X Band Radars operate at a higher frequency and provide a more detailed and accurate image of nearby objects. S Band Radars operate at a lower frequency and have a longer range, making them ideal for detecting objects at a distance. Both types of radar systems are essential for safe navigation on the water.</p></p>
