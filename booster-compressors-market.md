Single stage booster compressors have one compression stage, double stage booster compressors have two compression stages, and multi-stage booster compressors have three or more compression stages. Single stage compressors are suitable for lower pressure applications, while double and multi-stage compressors are used for higher pressure applications. Each type of booster compressor offers different levels of pressure boost and efficiency, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
