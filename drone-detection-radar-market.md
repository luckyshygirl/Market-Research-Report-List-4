<p><p>Drone detection radar market types include FMCW radar, Pulse-Doppler radar, and Other radar technologies. FMCW radar uses frequency-modulated continuous wave technology to continuously transmit and receive signals, providing accurate range and velocity measurements. Pulse-Doppler radar combines pulse radar with Doppler processing to detect moving targets, such as drones, amidst clutter. Other radar technologies in the market may include phased array radar or passive radar systems. These radar technologies are utilized to detect and track drones for security and defense purposes.</p></p>
