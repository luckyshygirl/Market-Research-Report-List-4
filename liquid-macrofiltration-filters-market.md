<p><p>Liquid macrofiltration filters include various types such as granular micro filters, leaf tubular and press filters, bag filters, belt filter presses, and other variations. Granular micro filters utilize small granules to remove particles from liquids, while leaf tubular and press filters use a series of tubes or plates to separate solids. Bag filters trap particles in a fabric bag, and belt filter presses use a belt system to squeeze liquid out of sludge. Other types of filters offer unique methods of macrofiltration for different applications.</p></p>
