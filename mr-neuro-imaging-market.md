<p><strong>The MR Neuro Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Superconducting</li><li>Permanent Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MR neuro imaging market can be divided into two main types based on the magnet technology used: superconducting magnets and permanent magnets. </p><p>Superconducting magnets offer higher magnetic field strength and image quality, making them the preferred choice for high-resolution neuro imaging. They require cooling systems to maintain superconducting state.</p><p>On the other hand, permanent magnets are simpler in design and do not require cryogenic cooling. They are more cost-effective and easier to maintain, making them suitable for lower-field neuro imaging applications.</p></p>
