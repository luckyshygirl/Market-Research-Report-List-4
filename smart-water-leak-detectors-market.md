<p><p>Passive leak detectors are devices that continuously monitor water flow and alert users of any abnormalities, such as sudden increases in flow rate indicating a possible leak. Active leak detectors, on the other hand, actively shut off the water supply when a leak is detected, preventing further damage. Both types of detectors play a crucial role in preventing water damage and reducing the risk of costly repairs, making them essential tools for homeowners and businesses looking to protect their property.</p></p>
