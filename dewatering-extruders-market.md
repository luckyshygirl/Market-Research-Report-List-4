<p><ul><li>Single Screw Extruder</li><li>Twin Screw Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dewatering extruders are used in various industries to remove excess moisture from materials. Single screw extruders have a simple design with one screw that rotates within a barrel to push material forward. Twin screw extruders have two intermeshing screws that work together to process materials more efficiently. Single screw extruders are commonly used for simple dewatering processes, while twin screw extruders are preferred for more complex applications requiring higher levels of moisture removal and mixing capabilities.</p></p>
