<p><p>The infant milk powder market consists of various types such as cow's milk-based formula, soy-based formula, protein hydrolysate-based formula, and specialty formula. Cow's milk-based formula is the most common type, while soy-based formula is suitable for infants with cow's milk allergies. Protein hydrolysate-based formula is designed for infants with digestion issues. Specialty formulas are tailored for specific dietary or medical needs such as lactose intolerance or premature birth. Each type caters to different requirements and preferences in infant nutrition.</p></p>
