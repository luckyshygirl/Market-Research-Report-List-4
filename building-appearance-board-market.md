<p><p>Building Appearance Board market is segmented into various types such as Block Board, Plywood, Medium Density Fiberboard, Particle Board, and Others. Block Board is made up of softwood strips sandwiched between hardwood veneers. Plywood is made from thin layers of wood glued together. Medium Density Fiberboard is a engineered wood product made by breaking down hardwood or softwood residuals into fibers. Particle Board is made from wood particles bonded together. Other types of boards include oriented strand board (OSB) and hardboard.</p></p>
