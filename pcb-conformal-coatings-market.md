<p><p>PCB conformal coatings are applied to protect printed circuit boards from environmental factors such as moisture, dust, and chemicals. The five main types of conformal coatings are Silicone, Parylene, Acrylic, Urethane, and Epoxy. Silicone coatings offer flexibility and high temperature resistance, Parylene is a thin and pinhole-free coating, Acrylic is easy to apply and remove, Urethane provides excellent chemical resistance, and Epoxy offers strong adhesion and durability. Each type of coating has specific advantages and is chosen based on the requirements of the application.</p></p>
