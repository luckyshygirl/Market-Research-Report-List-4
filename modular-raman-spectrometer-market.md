<p><strong>The Modular Raman Spectrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Coupling Modular Raman Spectrometer</li><li>Fiber Optic Modular Raman Spectrometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct Coupling Modular Raman Spectrometer is a type of Raman spectrometer where the laser source and detector are directly coupled to the analyzer unit without the need for fiber optics. This configuration provides higher sensitivity and resolution for Raman spectroscopy measurements. </p><p>On the other hand, Fiber Optic Modular Raman Spectrometer uses fiber optics to transmit the excitation laser light and collect the scattered Raman signal from the sample. This allows for more flexible and remote measurements in various samples and environments.</p></p>
