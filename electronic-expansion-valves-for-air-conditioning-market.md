<p><p>Electronic Expansion Valves (EEVs) for air conditioning systems come in two main types: Electromagnetic EEVs and Electric EEVs. Electromagnetic EEVs use a solenoid coil to control the flow of refrigerant, while Electric EEVs use a stepper motor for the same purpose. Both types of EEVs provide precise control of refrigerant flow, allowing for improved system efficiency and performance. The market for these electronic expansion valves is rapidly growing, driven by the increasing demand for energy-efficient air conditioning systems.</p></p>
