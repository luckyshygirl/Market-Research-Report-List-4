Solvent ink printers are used for outdoor applications due to their durability. UV-cured ink printers are known for quick drying times and scratch resistance. Latex ink printers are environmentally friendly and produce vibrant colors. Dye sublimation ink printers are commonly used for producing high-quality graphics on materials such as fabric and ceramics. Each ink type caters to different printing requirements and applications.</p></p>
