<p><p>Pure cheese powder is made solely from cheese, usually cheddar or parmesan, and is commonly used as a flavoring or topping for snacks, popcorn, and pasta dishes. Cheese powder blends, on the other hand, are a mixture of cheese powder with other ingredients like herbs, spices, or even additional dairy products. These blends offer a more complex and unique flavor profile compared to pure cheese powder, making them popular choices for seasoning mixes, dip mixes, and sauces.</p></p>
