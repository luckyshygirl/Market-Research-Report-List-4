<p><p>Antenna positioner market includes various types such as Azimuth Positioner, Vertical XY Positioner, Horizontal XY Positioner, and Others. Azimuth Positioners are used to rotate antennas horizontally, Vertical XY Positioners allow antennas to move vertically and horizontally simultaneously, Horizontal XY Positioners enable antennas to move only in horizontal directions. Other types may include polar mount positioners, elevation over azimuth positioners, or custom designs based on specific requirements. Each type of positioner serves a unique purpose in ensuring precise and accurate antenna alignment.</p></p>
