<p><p>On-premise airline reservation systems are deployed and managed on the airline's own servers, requiring in-house IT staff to maintain and update the system. Web-based reservation systems are hosted on the cloud, allowing airlines to access and manage the system remotely through a web browser. Web-based systems provide greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness compared to on-premise systems. Both types of systems offer features such as booking management, seat selection, payment processing, and itinerary generation to streamline the reservation process for airlines and passengers.</p></p>
