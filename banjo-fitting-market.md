<p><p>Banjo fittings come in various types, including Linear, Elbow, T Shape, and Y Shape. Linear banjo fittings are straight and direct, making them suitable for simple installations. Elbow banjo fittings have a right-angle bend, allowing for easy connection in tight spaces. T Shape banjo fittings branch out in different directions, ideal for branching off multiple lines. Y Shape banjo fittings split into two directions, commonly used for splitting or combining fluid flow. Each type serves a specific purpose in various industries.</p></p>
