<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway coupling market can be classified into three types based on their operation: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. In manual coupling, the operators physically connect individual railcars using tools and manpower. Semi-automatic coupling involves the use of mechanical aids to assist in the coupling process, reducing the manual effort required. Automatic coupling, on the other hand, relies on sensors and technology to connect railcars without human intervention, ensuring efficient and reliable coupling operations. Each type offers its own set of benefits and considerations for railway operators.</p></p>
