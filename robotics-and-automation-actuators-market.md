<p><p>Robotics and Automation Actuators market consists of three main types: Electric Actuators, Pneumatic Actuators, and Hydraulic Actuators. Electric actuators use electricity to create motion, making them precise and efficient. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to generate motion, making them versatile and cost-effective. Hydraulic actuators use hydraulic fluid to produce motion, making them capable of handling heavy loads. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in the robotics and automation industry.</p></p>
