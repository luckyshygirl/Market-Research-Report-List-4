<p><p>Rubber (or band) Powered spearguns use rubber bands to store energy and propel a spear towards the target. They are popular among beginner and recreational divers for their simplicity and ease of use. Air Powered (Pneumatic) spearguns utilize compressed air to launch the spear, offering more power and accuracy compared to rubber powered spearguns. They are favored by experienced divers and professional spearfishers for their high performance capabilities. Both types cater to different skill levels and preferences within the speargun market.</p></p>
