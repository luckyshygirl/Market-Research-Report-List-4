Double Shielded TBMs have an additional shield at the rear of the machine to support the tunnel face and prevent collapse. Single Shielded TBMs are typically used in stable ground conditions, while Double Shielded TBMs are used in more difficult ground conditions where additional support is necessary. Both types are essential for efficient and safe tunnel excavation.</p></p>
