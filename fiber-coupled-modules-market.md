<p><strong>The Fiber Coupled Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emitter-Based FCM</li><li>Bar-Based FCM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Coupled Modules (FCM) are commonly categorized into two types - Emitter-Based FCM and Bar-Based FCM. Emitter-Based FCM utilizes a single emitter to couple light into the fiber, providing compact and efficient solutions for various applications. On the other hand, Bar-Based FCM utilizes a laser bar to achieve higher power levels and wider applications. Both types of FCM offer unique advantages and are used in different industries based on their specific requirements for power, efficiency, and performance.</p></p>
